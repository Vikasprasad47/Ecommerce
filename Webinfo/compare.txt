❌ DISADVANTAGES OF YOUR PLATFORM (REAL, PRACTICAL, BUSINESS & TECH)

I’m listing them in descending seriousness, starting from the most important challenges.

🚨 1. You Depend Entirely on Sellers for Product Quality & Speed

Because you do not keep inventory:

You CANNOT control:

Packaging standards

Delivery times

Product quality

Fake/duplicate items

Stock accuracy

Return handling

Order delays

Platforms like Amazon, Blinkit, Zepto dominate because they control everything.

Your challenge:

📌 Fix later:

Strict seller rating system

Penalties for delays

Quality score

“Preferred Seller” program

Seller training program

🚨 2. Lower Control = Lower Customer Trust (Initially)

Platform-managed disputes

Fast refund guarantee

Partner with local courier fleets
